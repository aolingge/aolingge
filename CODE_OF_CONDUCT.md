# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This repository is a public profile and project index. We want it to stay
useful, respectful, and easy to maintain.

By participating here, you agree to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on actionable feedback, reproducible issues, and clear suggestions.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing personal, secret, or private workspace data.
- Keep discussion relevant to the projects and workflows listed in this profile.

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Spam, self-promotion without context, or repetitive low-signal comments.
- Sharing secrets, tokens, private links, credentials, or copied private notes.
- Requests to publish unsafe automation, bypass platform rules, or expose other
  people's data.

## Reporting

If you notice a problem, open a GitHub issue with enough context to review it,
or contact the maintainer directly through the contact methods listed in the
profile README.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, or pull
requests that do not follow this Code of Conduct.
